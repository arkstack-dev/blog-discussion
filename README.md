# 📝 ArkStack Dev Blog Discussions

Welcome to the official discussion and comment engine for [blog.arkstack.dev](https://blog.arkstack.dev).

This repository serves as the backend for the comments section on our blog articles. It’s a space to discuss software architecture, developer productivity, industry trends, and the journey of building deep-tech startups.

## 💬 How it Works

* **Read and React:** When you leave a comment at the bottom of an article on our blog, it is automatically synced as a discussion thread in this repository.
* **Continue the Debate:** You can reply directly on the blog or come here to read the full threads and interact with other readers via GitHub.

## 📜 Community Guidelines

* **Stay on Topic:** Keep comments relevant to the article you are discussing.
* **Be Constructive:** We encourage healthy debate and sharing different perspectives on architecture and coding practices.
* **Share Your Experience:** Did you solve a similar problem differently? Drop your insights and code snippets!
